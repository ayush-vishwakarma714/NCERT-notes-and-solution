Shapes Around Us

This chapter helps children identify different shapes found around us.

📖 Chapter Notes

🔺 Different Shapes

We see many shapes around us.

  • Circle
  • Square
  • Rectangle
  • Triangle

Common shapes around us

Think & Answer

Q. Name any one shape.

Answer: Circle.

🏠 Shapes in Real Life

Objects around us have shapes.

  • Ball – Circle
  • Book – Rectangle
  • Window – Square
  • Pizza slice – Triangle

Shapes in daily life

Think & Answer

Q. What is the shape of a ball?

Answer: Circle.
